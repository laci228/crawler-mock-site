The City Museum offers a deep dive into ExploreCity's development from a small trading post to a vibrant metropolis. It features multimedia exhibits and hands-on activities for all ages.

Exhibits

  • Prehistoric artifacts
  • Urban planning and architecture
  • Art and culture of the 20th century

Visitor Information

Open Tuesday to Sunday, 10am to 5pm. Closed on Mondays. Admission: $8. Free on the first Sunday of every month.
