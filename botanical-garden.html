Botanical Garden

← Back to Home

The Botanical Garden is a haven of biodiversity, home to over 5,000 plant species from around the globe. It offers educational displays, serene walking paths, and seasonal flower festivals.

Features

  • Tropical greenhouse
  • Japanese Zen garden
  • Interactive children's area

Visitor Information

Open daily from 8am to 7pm. Entry: $6. Free guided tours available on weekends.
