Historic Castle

The Historic Castle stands as a testament to ExploreCity's rich past. Built in the 12th century, it has served as a royal residence, fortress, and cultural symbol throughout the ages.

Highlights

  • Guided historical tours
  • Stunning city views from the tower
  • Annual medieval fair with reenactments

Visitor Information

Open daily from 9am to 6pm. Entry fee: $10 (discounts available for students and seniors).
